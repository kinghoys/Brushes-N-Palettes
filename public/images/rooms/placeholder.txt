For the Art Visualization tool to work properly, you need to add the following room images to this directory:

1. living-room.jpg - A modern living room with empty wall space for artwork
2. bedroom.jpg - A bedroom setting with wall space above the bed or on side walls
3. office.jpg - A home office with desk and wall space for art
4. dining.jpg - A dining room with empty wall space

Recommended image sizes: 1200x800 pixels or larger with 16:9 aspect ratio.

These images should be royalty-free or owned by you to avoid copyright issues.

Good sources for room images:
- Unsplash.com
- Pexels.com
- Your own interior photography
